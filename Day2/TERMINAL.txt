  Linux
  the operating system manages the communication between 
  your software and your hardware. Without the operating system (OS), the software wouldn’t function.

  Linux is an open-source, community-developed operating system
   with the kernel at its core, alongside other tools, applications, and services.

The kernel interfaces the operating system and the underlying 
hardware and facilitates communication between the two.

Process Management – The kernel manages running processes in order to avoid conflicts and deadlocks 
and also for the optimal functioning of the system.
